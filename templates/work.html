Sentiment analysis is the utmost progressive research field in natural language processing that focuses on identifying the attitude, opinion, feelings and emotions of people towards entities and their facets. The social networks, forums, content sharing services and micro-blogging sites holds valuable and vast information about public opinion and can be considered as data sources in sentiment analysis process.
In this work, Twitter was regarded as the data source and TWINT (Twitter intelligence tool), an unconventional python programmed Twitter scraping tool was utilized to scrape 1500 (710 with depressive sentiment and 790 with non-depressive sentiment) tweets.Thereafter, the acquired raw data was cleaned using regular expression(re) library and further, was transformed by applying CountVectorizer and TfidfTransformer.
